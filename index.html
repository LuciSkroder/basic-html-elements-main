Recipe for PB & J

A simple and delicious sandwhich. A true lunch classic.

The finished product.

Ingredients

  • Sliced bread
  • Peanut butter
  • Jelly or jam

Instructions

  1. Lay out two slices of bread
  2. Spread peanut butter evenely across one side of one bread
  3. Spread jelly evenely across one side of the other bread
  4. Press the two slices of bread together
